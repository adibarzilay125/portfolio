Animation as part of a music video (coming soon)

A short animated sequence created for an upcoming music video Pina by the band Shen.

The animation follows a figure running toward the moon, until they eventually merge and become one. It appears at the final part of the song, where I wanted to create a sense of closure, a moment in which the character, the moon, and the universe come together as one.
