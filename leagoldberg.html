Lea Goldberg: An Inner Dialogue

A poster exploring the internal relationship of Lea Goldberg with herself, offering a perspective that diverges from the familiar narratives associated with her work. The project reinterprets her emotional and intellectual world through a contemporary lens, focusing on introspection, self-dialogue, and the tension between public identity and private experience. Through visual language and composition, the poster seeks to reveal a more intimate and less conventional portrayal of the poet.
