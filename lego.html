Visual Identity for a Fictional LEGO Competition Event

A series of posters created for a fictional event centered around the country’s largest LEGO competition. The project explores the development of a cohesive visual language, combining playful elements with a structured design system. Through typography, color, and composition, the work aims to capture the excitement, creativity, and competitive spirit of the event, while maintaining a clear and engaging visual identity across all materials.
