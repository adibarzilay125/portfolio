Interactive game exploring focus and the perception of time

An interactive game that simulates the experience of trying to stay focused while dealing with constant inner distractions. The interaction is inspired by the way the mind can work for a person with attention difficulties struggling to “turn off” thoughts, comments, and mental interruptions while trying to complete a task. In the game, focus affects time: when the player manages to stay focused, time moves faster and the task progresses. When focus is lost and the inner distractions take over, the player has to start again, making the task feel longer and slower. Once the player succeeds in maintaining focus, the task is completed successfully.
